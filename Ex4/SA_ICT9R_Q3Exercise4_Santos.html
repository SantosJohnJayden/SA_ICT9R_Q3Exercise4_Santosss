Vigan - Philipines

Vigan is a city in the Philippines located at the west coast of Luzon Island, the city is considered as one of the oldest towns in the Philippines, Mainly because of the preservation of its Hispanic colonial nature. The old city is known for their cobblestone streets, European architecture that combines Philippine and oriental building designs and construction, which makes it so unique.

Activities in Vigan:

Visiting the Large Church, Bell tower, Calesa Horse rides, Dancing Fountain and etc.
